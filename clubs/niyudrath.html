TEAM NIYUDRATH RACING (TNR)

AUTOSPORTS CLUB

Description

Team Niyudrath Racing is the official autosports club of MITAOE, dedicated to building and racing student-designed vehicles. The club focuses on practical automotive engineering skills and motorsports competitions.

Staff Co-ordinators

Mr. Rohan Patil, Mechanical Department

Social Media Handles

Vision

To foster a passion for motorsports and innovation, developing practical automotive skills through real-world competitions.

Mission

Enhancing students' technical and management skills by designing and building competitive racing vehicles.

Objectives

Participating in national and international racing events, promoting teamwork, and improving engineering skills.
